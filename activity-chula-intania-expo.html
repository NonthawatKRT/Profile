Exhibition Hackathon Partnership

CHULA Intania Expo & Thailand Metaverse Hackathon

March 28, 2025 • Exhibition & Competition

About the Event

Participated in the prestigious Intania Expo at Chulalongkorn University's Faculty of Engineering, showcasing SWU Metaverse at the Thailand Metaverse hackathon. This event brought together top metaverse developers and innovators from across Thailand.

Our participation led to a significant partnership: we became official partners of Mangosgo.com, the metaverse platform developed by Chulalongkorn University, marking an important milestone in our metaverse development journey.

Key Achievements

  • Successfully exhibited SWU Metaverse at one of Thailand's premier engineering events
  • Competed in Thailand Metaverse hackathon
  • Established partnership with Mangosgo.com (Chulalongkorn University's metaverse platform)
  • Networked with leading metaverse developers and researchers
  • Demonstrated technical capabilities to engineering faculty and students

Impact

This event was pivotal in establishing our credibility in Thailand's metaverse development community. The partnership with Mangosgo.com opened doors for future collaborations and positioned SWU Metaverse as a key player in the Thai educational metaverse landscape.
