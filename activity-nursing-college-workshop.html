Workshop Healthcare Metaverse

Metaverse Workshop at Boromarajonani College of Nursing

August 16, 2025 • Educational Workshop

Workshop Overview

A comprehensive training workshop for Boromarajonani College of Nursing to learn and understand metaverse technology. The goal was to empower each nursing college to create their own virtual campus and integrate educational activities and knowledge into their custom metaverse environments for enhanced healthcare education.

Workshop Content

  • Introduction to metaverse technology and its applications
  • Metaverse platform overview and navigation
  • Creating and customizing virtual campus environments
  • Integrating educational activities into metaverse spaces
  • Hands-on practice with metaverse creation tools

My Contributions

Joined the instructor team from Chulalongkorn University to conduct this specialized training workshop. Provided technical demonstrations and guided nursing college staff through the process of creating and customizing their own metaverse environments for healthcare education applications.
