About the Event

Participated as part of Srinakharinwirot University team at Thailand Inventor's Day 2026, one of the nation's premier innovation showcases. Our team operated an activity booth in the highlight zone, featuring the SWU-Metaverse VR game experience.

This event brought together inventors, researchers, and students from across Thailand to demonstrate cutting-edge innovations and creative solutions. Our booth attracted numerous visitors who experienced the immersive virtual campus environment we developed.

My Role

  • Developed and updated SWU-Metaverse specifically for the Inventor's Day exhibition
  • Demonstrated SWU-Metaverse VR game to visitors and attendees
  • Explained the technical implementation and game features
  • Assisted visitors in experiencing the VR gameplay
  • Represented Srinakharinwirot University at the highlight zone booth
