Metaverse Workshop at Bangkok University

October 22, 2025 • Educational Workshop

Workshop Overview

Conducted metaverse training and workshop for students at Bangkok University as part of the lecturers team collaborating with Chula metaverse. This workshop at one of Thailand's prominent private universities focused on introducing metaverse development and applications to tech-savvy students.

Workshop Content

  • Comprehensive introduction to metaverse platforms and technologies
  • Virtual world design and development principles
  • Practical demonstrations of metaverse creation tools
  • Group activities and collaborative virtual space creation
  • Career opportunities in metaverse development

My Contributions

Joined the instructor team from Chulalongkorn University to conduct this metaverse training workshop for Bangkok University students. Provided technical demonstrations, guided hands-on activities, and shared insights on metaverse development and career opportunities in the field.

Featured News

This workshop was featured on Bangkok University's official website, highlighting the collaboration between universities to advance metaverse education in Thailand.
