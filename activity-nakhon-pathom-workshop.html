Metaverse Workshop at Nakhon Pathom Rajabhat University

June 29, 2025 • Educational Workshop

Workshop Overview

Conducted metaverse training and workshop for students at Nakhon Pathom Rajabhat University as part of the lecturers team collaborating with Chula metaverse. This workshop aimed to introduce university students to metaverse development and its educational applications.

Training Content

  • Metaverse fundamentals and platform overview
  • Virtual world creation and design principles
  • Interactive demonstrations of educational metaverse applications
  • Hands-on activities with metaverse tools
  • Q&A and discussion about future educational opportunities

My Contributions

Joined the instructor team from Chulalongkorn University to conduct this training workshop. Served as the main lecturer for the Unity game development session, demonstrating game creation techniques and guiding students through hands-on metaverse development exercises.
