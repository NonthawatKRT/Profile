Exhibition Showcase SWU-Metaverse

SCI POWER FOR FUTURE THAILAND

August 11, 2025 • Public Exhibition

Event Overview

Participated in the SCI POWER FOR FUTURE THAILAND event as a representative of the Srinakharinwirot University team, showcasing and demonstrating the SWU-Metaverse project to the public. This event attracted visitors from various backgrounds, providing an excellent opportunity to present our work to a diverse audience.

Exhibition Activities

  • Set up and managed the SWU-Metaverse exhibition booth
  • Provided live demonstrations of the metaverse platform
  • Engaged with visitors and answered questions about the project
  • Represented Srinakharinwirot University's innovation capabilities
  • Collected feedback and insights from diverse audience members

Public Engagement

The SCI POWER FOR FUTURE THAILAND event provided valuable experience in presenting technical projects to a general audience. It challenged us to explain complex metaverse concepts in accessible terms and highlighted the broad interest in virtual world technologies beyond academic circles.
