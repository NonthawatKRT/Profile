Metaverse Workshop at I-Nam Cafe & Bar, Amphawa

December 5, 2025 • Community Workshop

Unique Workshop Setting

Joined the lecturers team with Chula metaverse to conduct metaverse training workshop at I-Nam Cafe & Bar in the historic Amphawa district of Samut Songkhram, Thailand. This unique venue brought metaverse technology to a community setting, reaching beyond traditional academic environments.

Workshop Highlights

  • Introduced metaverse concepts to local community members and visitors
  • Demonstrated practical applications of virtual worlds for small businesses
  • Explored potential for virtual tourism and cultural preservation
  • Provided hands-on experience with metaverse platforms
  • Connected traditional Thai culture with modern technology

My Contributions

Joined the instructor team from Chulalongkorn University to conduct this community workshop. Served as Lead Unity Developer, responsible for directing the team in developing all core systems for the I-Nam Cafe metaverse. Key achievements included:

  • Led Unity development team to create the I-Nam Cafe virtual environment
  • Developed an interactive cooking mini-game system
  • Implemented exploration mechanics allowing players to walk around the cafe garden
  • Created ingredient collection system for gathering cooking materials
  • Integrated game mechanics with the cafe's actual menu and recipes
