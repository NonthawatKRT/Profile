VR Game Development Workshop at SWU

April 24, 2025 • Educational Workshop

Workshop Overview

Joined the university lecturers team to conduct comprehensive VR game development training and workshop for students at Srinakharinwirot University. This hands-on workshop introduced students to the fundamentals of virtual reality development and game design principles.

Topics Covered

  • Introduction to VR game development and hardware
  • Unity VR development fundamentals
  • VR interaction design and user experience
  • Hands-on VR project development
  • Best practices for VR performance optimization

My Contributions

As part of the teaching team, I assisted in curriculum development, provided technical demonstrations, and mentored students during hands-on coding sessions. This experience strengthened both my technical skills and ability to communicate complex concepts effectively.
