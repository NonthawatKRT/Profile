VR Caring on Spatial.io

July 3, 2025 - Ongoing • Cross-Platform VR Game

Project Overview

VR Caring is a cross-platform game developed on Spatial.io, designed to preserve and share Thai traditional culture and nostalgia. The game is accessible on mobile, PC, and VR platforms, making Thai cultural heritage accessible to elderly users and anyone interested in traditional Thai experiences.

Featured Minigames

  • Traditional Thai Desserts Cooking Game: Learn and experience the process of making authentic Thai sweets
  • Traditional Thai Dressing Game: Explore and wear various Thai traditional costumes and accessories
  • Boat Trip to Wat Chaiwatthanaram: Navigate scenic waterways to the historic temple
  • Thai Floating Market Experience: Explore and interact in a virtual traditional floating market

My Role

Served as Lead Unity Developer, responsible for developing all core game systems including:

  • Player data management system
  • Character customization and dressing system
  • All minigame mechanics and interactions
  • Automatic navigation and pathfinding system
  • Cross-platform optimization to ensure smooth performance on Mobile, PC, and VR platforms

Development Highlights

This project challenged me to create culturally authentic experiences while ensuring accessibility across multiple platforms. The cross-platform nature required careful optimization and design considerations to maintain performance and immersion on mobile, PC, and VR devices.

Technologies Used

Spatial.io VR Unity Cross-Platform 3D Modeling
