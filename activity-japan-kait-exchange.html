Japan Exchange Program with KAIT University

July 22, 2025 • International Collaboration

About the Program

Selected as a university representative to visit Japan as part of an exclusive international collaboration program. Joined the Chula metaverse team to work with KAIT University (Kanagawa Institute of Technology) on metaverse development and knowledge exchange.

This prestigious opportunity allowed me to collaborate with Japanese students and researchers, share our SWU-Metaverse development experience, and learn about cutting-edge metaverse technologies and approaches being developed in Japan.

Activities & Achievements

  • Presented SWU-Metaverse project to KAIT University faculty and students
  • Participated in collaborative metaverse development workshops
  • Exchanged knowledge on VR game development techniques and best practices
  • Explored potential future collaboration opportunities between institutions
  • Experienced Japanese game development culture and methodologies

Key Learnings

This international experience broadened my perspective on game development and metaverse technologies. I gained insights into different development approaches, collaborative workflows, and the global landscape of virtual world creation.
